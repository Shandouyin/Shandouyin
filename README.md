## About me

I grew up watching sci-fi movies and wondering how much of it could actually become real. Turns out, a lot of it already is — which is probably why I ended up in AI and Data Science at **UTBM**.

Over the past few years I've worked on things that genuinely excited me: training agents to play Pac-Man, generating Pokémon images with diffusion models, predicting failures and detecting anomalies on NASA datasets. Some of it was messy, most of it required a lot of debugging, but that's what I enjoy — figuring things out until they work.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I play guitar, travel whenever I get the chance, hike, and spend probably too much time watching kdramas and anime. I like projects that have a real purpose and people who care about what they build.

Currently looking for a **6-month international internship starting September 2026** — if you're working on something interesting, feel free to reach out.
